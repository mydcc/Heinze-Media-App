While traditional platforms store sensitive data like API keys and trade histories on central servers, Cachy utilizes a radical **Local-First architecture**.

* **Absolute Data Sovereignty**: All settings, API keys, and journal entries remain exclusively in your browser's local storage (`localStorage`). There is no central database and no registration required.
* **Risk-Centric Trading**: Cachy works "backwards." Instead of asking "how much do I want to buy?", you define your risk amount in dollars. The system then mathematically calculates the optimal position size based on your stop-loss.
