<div class="wp-block-uagb-container uagb-block-c288c559"><!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>AI (Artificial Intelligence)</strong><br>The ability of computer systems to perform human-like intelligence processes such as learning, problem solving and decision making.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Immersive experiences</strong><br>Experiences that fully immerse the user in a simulated environment, often using technologies such as VR, AR and XR.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Digital twins</strong><br>Virtual replicas of physical objects, systems or processes that are used for simulation and analysis.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Superapps</strong><br>Mobile applications that bundle a variety of services and functions into a single platform.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>XR (Extended Reality)</strong><br>An umbrella term for immersive technologies that combine the real and virtual worlds, including VR, AR and MR.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>VR (Virtual Reality)</strong><br>A technology that immerses the user in a fully computer-generated environment, typically using a headset.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Industry cloud platforms</strong><br>Cloud-basierte Plattformen, die auf die spezifischen Anforderungen einer bestimmten Branche zugeschnitten sind.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>AR (Augmented Reality)<br></strong>A technology that superimposes digital content onto the real world, typically using a smartphone or tablet.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Metaverse</strong><br>A network of persistent, immersive virtual worlds and simulations in which users can interact and create content together.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>ROI (Return on Investment)<br></strong>A measure of the profitability of an investment.</p>
