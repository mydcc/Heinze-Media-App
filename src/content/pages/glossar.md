# Glossar

**AI (Artificial Intelligence)**  
The ability of computer systems to perform human-like intelligence processes such as learning, problem solving and decision making.

**Immersive experiences**  
Experiences that fully immerse the user in a simulated environment, often using technologies such as VR, AR and XR.

**Digital twins**  
Virtual replicas of physical objects, systems or processes that are used for simulation and analysis.

**Superapps**  
Mobile applications that bundle a variety of services and functions into a single platform.

**XR (Extended Reality)**  
An umbrella term for immersive technologies that combine the real and virtual worlds, including VR, AR and MR.

**VR (Virtual Reality)**  
A technology that immerses the user in a fully computer-generated environment, typically using a headset.

**Industry cloud platforms**  
Cloud-basierte Plattformen, die auf die spezifischen Anforderungen einer bestimmten Branche zugeschnitten sind.

**AR (Augmented Reality)  
**A technology that superimposes digital content onto the real world, typically using a smartphone or tablet.

**Metaverse**  
A network of persistent, immersive virtual worlds and simulations in which users can interact and create content together.

**ROI (Return on Investment)  
**A measure of the profitability of an investment.
