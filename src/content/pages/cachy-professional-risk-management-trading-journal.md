<div class="wp-block-uagb-container uagb-block-9123be77"><!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Cachy is a high-performance trading companion designed to bridge the gap between professional trading terminals and user-friendly portfolio trackers. At its core, Cachy is built on the philosophy that successful trading doesn't start with order execution, but with consistent capital preservation.</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">üõ°Ô∏è The Philosophy: Money First, Privacy Always</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>While traditional platforms store sensitive data like API keys and trade histories on central servers, Cachy utilizes a radical <strong>Local-First architecture</strong>.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Absolute Data Sovereignty</strong>: All settings, API keys, and journal entries remain exclusively in your browser's local storage (<code>localStorage</code>). There is no central database and no registration required.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Risk-Centric Trading</strong>: Cachy works "backwards." Instead of asking "how much do I want to buy?", you define your risk amount in dollars. The system then mathematically calculates the optimal position size based on your stop-loss.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">‚ú® Core Features at a Glance</h2>

<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Precision to the Cent</strong>: By integrating <code>Decimal.js</code>, Cachy avoids floating-point errors to ensure financial calculations are performed with absolute accuracy.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Dynamic Stop-Loss</strong>: Use the integrated ATR (Average True Range) calculation to automatically adjust your stop-loss based on current market volatility.</li>

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Multi-Target Take Profit</strong>: Plan partial exits for up to 5 targets with automatic rebalancing and detailed Risk/Reward (R/R) analysis.</li>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading">üìì Deep-Dive Trading Journal</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Cachy is more than just a logbook; it is an analysis engine for your trading behavior:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Professional Metrics</strong>: Track key performance indicators such as the <strong>Profit Factor</strong>, <strong>SQN</strong> (System Quality Number), and <strong>MAE/MFE</strong> to measure your exit efficiency.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Behavioral Analysis</strong>: Identify "profit leaks" through time-based analysis (days of the week/hours) or recognize psychological patterns like "tilt" after losing streaks.</li>

<li><strong>Strategy Attribution</strong>: Tag your trades (e.g., "Breakout" or "FOMO") to discover which setups statistically provide the highest returns for you.</li>
